About us

Our history

Maxx Energy was founded in 2010 by a group of passionate environmentalists and engineers who wanted to make a difference. We started with a small solar panel installation project in a local neighborhood and quickly grew as more people saw the benefits of clean energy. Today, we serve thousands of homes and communities, helping to reduce carbon footprints and promote sustainable living.

Our mission

Our mission is simple: to provide affordable, clean energy solutions that benefit both our customers and the planet. We believe in a future where renewable energy is the norm, not the exception. By harnessing the power of the sun, wind, and other renewable sources, we're working to create a cleaner, greener world for generations to come.

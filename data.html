Maxx Energy Data Insights

Decline in Wind and Solar Power Costs

According to the International Renewable Energy Agency (IRENA), the cost of solar photovoltaic (PV) modules has declined by 90% since 2010, making solar energy more affordable than ever.

Wind power costs have also decreased significantly. Onshore wind costs have fallen by around 60% since 2010, further enhancing the economic viability of renewable energy sources.

Negative Impact of Fossil Fuels

Fossil fuels contribute to air and water pollution, leading to health problems and environmental degradation. According to the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA), burning fossil fuels for electricity is the largest single source of CO2 emissions in the United States.

The shift towards clean energy not only mitigates these environmental impacts but also provides sustainable solutions for our future energy needs.
